Server Features

Levels & Skills

Progression system with skill XP and level-ups that unlock perks and titles.

Custom Mobs & Drops

Unique drops and rewards from custom mobs tailored to each cave and zone.

Emerald Slots & Economy

Hunting has a chance to drop emeralds. Exchange them for items or cosmetics. Balanced, no pay-to-win.

Hunting Caves & Set Gear

Explore themed caves with equipment sets designed for that cave. Face mini-bosses and bosses for big rewards.

Trade, No Auction House

We promote face-to-face trading with a secure trade system—no server-wide AH spam.

PvP Arena & Sets

Dedicated PvP area with unlockable sets tuned for PvP. Earn your kit and prove it in the arena.

Cosmetics

Particles, hats, trails, emotes, and kits—visual flair only.

Player Shops

Limited chest shops: players place chests to create NPC-like trades in-world—tight and community-driven.

Skyblock Islands

Four island types with configurable settings. Build solo or invite friends and grow your world.

Timed Rewards

Log-in activity triggers timed rewards that keep play sessions engaging.

Quests & NPCs

Simple turn-in quests, interactive NPCs with tips, trades, and story flavor.

Pets & Companions

Collect pets to follow you around—purely cosmetic and very cute.

Live Moderation & Staff Tools

Active staff, clear rules, and tools to keep the server safe, fair, and fun.

Crates from Gameplay

Earn keys through play. Open crates for cosmetics, materials, and fun bonuses—never pay-to-win.
